Bio

Pooja has spent the last 7 years understanding and fulfilling the needs of a variety of clients and customers, most recently at a London-based Technology Startup. She is fully committed to developing her knowledge through pursuing a long-term career in UX Design. She has been expanding her skillset at both Startup Institute and CareerFoundry. She loves going to UX Design meetups in Berlin to meet and be inspired by like-minded people. She's always ready to take on a new challenge, and is at her most happiest collaborating in a team.

"I love to understand what makes people tick and using that knowledge to build meaningful and engaging experiences."
